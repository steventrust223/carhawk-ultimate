CarHawk Ultimate - Help

Getting Started

1. Initialize System

Go to CarHawk Ultimate > Quantum Operations > Initialize System to set up all required sheets and configurations.

2. Import Deals

Use Quantum Sync to import deals from Browse.ai integrations, or manually add deals to the Master Import sheet.

3. Run AI Analysis

Execute AI Analysis from the Quantum Operations menu to analyze deals and generate verdicts.

4. Export to CRM

Export hot deals to SMS-iT or CompanyHub from the CRM & Export menu.

Key Features

Deal Gallery

Visual gallery of your top deals with quick action buttons.

Lead Management

Track leads through your pipeline from import to close.

Turo Module

Analyze deals for Turo rental potential and manage your fleet.
